Ongoing Asphalt Maintenance Program: Asphalt Maintenance Consulting

To implement an ongoing asphalt maintenance consulting program means to continue to strive to keep the pavement covered and the UV block in place. A maintenance program also means keeping the cracks sealed and the markings in attractive condition. This will result in maximum life out of your company's pavement. This is achieved by annual inspections and touching up areas such as cracks that need refilling, any new cracks that need to be filled or pavement that needs resealing. By now, you have control of your pavement and the maintenance cost drops significantly after the initial install, to approximately 25-30% of the original foundation, which is patching, crack-sealing, priming, sealcoating and line striping. We will monitor the pavement's wear points and address them as needed. Big savings!

About Us

Buyer Beware!

Our company was founded on a high standard of quality in both our workmanship and products. Being in business for 15 years has given us a chance to see a lot of contractors operating below standards, such as watering down sealer to get the job done cheaper, putting down crack sealers that are not designed for direct traffic and meant for overlay purposes only, not cleaning the surface properly or applying the correct surface primers all to be the lowest bidder. If your company were to hire a contractor like this, you would be very unsatisfied with the results and the quality.

In industry lingo, your company would have been "Gipsy-Juiced", meaning your contractor used watered-down sealer. In order for a sealer to perform properly, the molecular chain must be in place and tight-knit. If not, the sealer is nothing more than a black, short-lived masking (not to mention a waste of money.) We at Riz-Mat sealcoating will give you a spec based on all of the materials, which are detailed out in our contract. This serves as an insurance policy for your company. After all, we want to build a lasting relationship with our clients.

All of us here at Riz-Mat sealcoating look forward to servicing your company with a highest standard of quality. We hope you enjoy browsing our website and learn some of the aspects of asphalt maintenance. Feel free to contact us with any questions at (305)-901-0610.
